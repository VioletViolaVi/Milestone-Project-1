About Us


Our Founder

BusyBakes was founded by Mrs. Annalise W. Keating in a little cottage located in the middle of Hopestone Valley. Mrs Keating’s humble beginnings originate from her love of baking the likes of scones and cupcakes, with her grandchildren on cold rainy days. She enjoyed teaching her grandchildren all the tricks of the trade regarding how to bake exceptionally well. This blossomed into a deep passion for sharing her baking knowledge with others. So she started her business by holding classes in her kitchen, teaching her neighbours and their kids how to bake, before expanding it to a multi-million pound business. Thus, BusyBakes was born.


What makes us unique?

We pride ourselves as a leading in-class baking company that continuously receives positive feedback from our members and holds high customer retention. Not to mention our first place prized position in the National Online Baking Awards; clearly outcompeting our competitors. We offer exciting classes to both individuals and groups. We also succeed in being able to teach a wide range of people regardless of level. From beginners to experts. And are always rated first choice, by our beginner level members, who routinely express that BusyBakes is the best to sign up with if one wishes to master a new skill.


Why choose us?

We focus on providing an educational, fun and enriching experience for all our members. Our desire to share our love for baking with others makes us stand out from the crowd. We don’t just offer a class to teach you how to bake. It’s about more than baking. We build up a relationship with our members and help increase their confidence to think differently and try out new things. We relight an inner passion that had always been buried deep down inside and provide them the permission to explore it. We inspire. We empower. We accomplish.
